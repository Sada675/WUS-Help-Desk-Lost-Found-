📘 Guidelines & Instructions

Welcome to the Lost & Found System. Follow these guidelines for a smooth and safe experience.

📌 Instructions for You

  • Always use your real information while posting items.
  • Upload clear images of lost/found items.
  • Use chat or Gmail button only for genuine communication.
  • Mark item as Recovered/Returned after successful claim.
  • Do not spam or send unnecessary messages.

⚙️ How to Use

  1. Register and verify using OTP.
  2. Set your password.
  3. Report lost or found items.
  4. Search items using search bar.
  5. Contact users via Chat or Gmail.

⚠️ Warnings

  • Never post fake or misleading information.
  • Avoid unsafe physical meetings.
  • Respect all users.
  • Admin can take action on misuse.

💡 Best Practices

  • Provide exact location details.
  • Keep your profile updated.
  • Respond quickly to messages.
